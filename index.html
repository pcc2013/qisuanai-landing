Frequently Asked Questions

What is CogCloud AI?

CogCloud AI is a content operations platform that automates the full pipeline — from research and writing to video production and multi-channel publishing — across five vertical sites. On top of that infrastructure, we built Nira — 22 digital humans you can actually talk to. Voice, video, text. It's how we prove our technology works in the real world.

What is Nira?

Nira is our AI companion app with 22 unique digital human personas. Voice calls, video calls, and text chat — built on the same infrastructure that powers CogCloud AI. Free to start.

Can enterprises purchase private deployment?

We offer full private deployment packages, including the complete content production pipeline, dedicated compute resources, and ongoing technical support. Typical deployment is five business days.

Why is your compute cost lower?

We run our own GPU clusters, negotiate long-term model partnerships, and use local cache inference to bring costs down by 30% compared to standard cloud AI pricing.

Does Nira support custom digital humans?

Yes. Enterprise clients can commission custom AI personas and digital human avatars tailored to their brand or use case.
