Science facts: Earth orbits the Sun at about 107,000 km/h - yet our senses detect none of this motion. Sunlight takes about 8 minutes and 20 seconds to travel from the Sun's surface to Earth - meaning the Sun you see is always the Sun of 8 minutes ago. If all DNA molecules in a human body were stretched end to end, they could travel from Earth to the Sun and back about 600 times. Water is one of the very few substances that expands when freezing, allowing ice to float and protecting aquatic life beneath. Curiosity is humanity's most valuable scientific quality - every discovery began with someone asking "why" about something seemingly ordinary.
