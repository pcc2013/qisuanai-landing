Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

Nira subscriptions are processed via Stripe, accepting all major credit and debit cards. CogCloud plans are invoiced directly — contact us for payment terms. Enterprise deployments are invoiced with net-30 payment terms.

Is there a free trial for CogCloud?

We don't offer a self-serve free trial for CogCloud, but we do offer a guided demo with sample outputs from your target vertical. Contact sales to request one.

What does "Personal Digital Self" include?

The Personal Digital Self add-on lets you create a custom AI persona trained on your communication style, knowledge, and preferences. You provide photos, voice samples, and chat logs — all encrypted and processed locally. Raw materials are deleted within 7 days after the persona is created. Available as a $5/month add-on to Nira Pro.

Do I own the content CogCloud produces?

Yes. All content produced by CogCloud on your behalf — articles, videos, images, and metadata — is 100% owned by you. CogCloud does not retain, repurpose, or train on your content.

How fast is enterprise deployment?

Typical deployment completes within 5 business days. This includes vertical configuration, distribution channel setup, dedicated compute allocation, and initial content seeding.
