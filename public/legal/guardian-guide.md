# Nira Guardian Guide

**Last Updated: May 5, 2026**

## Dear Guardian,

A young person aged 15–17 is attempting to register for Nira, an AI emotional companion and digital human interaction platform.

Because this individual is under 18, and in compliance with our safety policies and applicable data protection laws, we require your informed consent before registration can proceed.

## 1. What Is Nira?

Nira offers the following features:
- **AI Text Chat**: Emotional conversations with AI characters
- **AI Voice Calls**: Voice conversations with AI characters

The following features are **not available** to users aged 15–17:
- Digital human video calls
- Exclusive Avatar creation (requires uploading facial photos and voice)
- Real-person video call matching

## 2. What Information Does Nira Collect?

Nira collects the following essential information:
- Email address (for account registration)
- Date of birth (for age verification)
- AI conversation history (users may delete at any time)

Nira does **not** collect:
- Precise geolocation
- Phone contacts
- Audio/video call recordings (not stored)

See the full Privacy Policy for details.

## 3. QS — Platform Internal Access Rights

Nira uses QS as a platform internal access right for feature billing. See the in-app "QS Pricing" page for current rates. QS is not a currency, security, or financial product.

**Important**: By completing this guardian verification, you consent to the minor user independently conducting QS transactions. You may not seek a refund of consumed QS on the grounds of the minor's lack of contractual capacity.

## 4. Dynamic Guardian Verification Steps

Our verification system uses dynamic knowledge-based questions to confirm your identity—this is more secure than a simple static password and prevents minors from impersonating guardians.

**Step 1**: The minor user provides your email address during registration.

**Step 2**: You receive a verification email from Nira (noreply@qisuanai.com).

**Step 3**: You must answer at least 2 out of 3 randomly generated questions. These questions are based on publicly available data associated with your email address. Examples:
- "Which of the following streets have you lived on?"
- "In which year was your phone number first registered?"
- "Which of the following companies have you been associated with?"

**Step 4**: After passing, you set a **6-digit PIN**.

**Step 5**: You communicate the PIN to the minor user (in person, by phone, or by message).

**Step 6**: The minor user enters the PIN in the app to complete registration.

**Anti-spoofing measures**:
- 3 incorrect answers → 30-minute lockout
- 5 cumulative incorrect answers → 24-hour lockout
- Your email and verification timestamp are recorded for audit purposes

**If you do not consent**: Do not complete the verification. The minor user will be unable to register.

## 5. Your Rights as a Guardian

You have the right to:
- **Request activity logs**: Review the minor user's activity at guardian@qisuanai.com (response within 7 business days)
- **Request account closure**: The minor user's account and all associated data will be deleted within 7 days
- **Correct information**: Correct any inaccurate personal data provided by the minor user
- **Withdraw consent**: Withdraw your consent at any time. The minor user's account will be closed upon withdrawal. Consumed QS is non-refundable
