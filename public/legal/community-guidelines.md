# Nira Community Guidelines

**Last Updated: May 5, 2026**

## Welcome to the Nira Community

Nira is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and inclusive space for AI companionship and social interaction for users worldwide. These Community Guidelines apply to all user conduct, including but not limited to: text chat, voice calls, digital human video calls, real-person video calls, and Exclusive Avatar creation.

## 1. Core Principles

1. **Respect others.** All users deserve respect regardless of nationality, race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, or age.
2. **Be honest.** Do not impersonate others, fabricate identities, or spread false information.
3. **Follow the law.** Comply with the laws and regulations of your country and region.
4. **Protect privacy.** Do not record, screenshot, or share others' personal information during calls.

## 2. Strictly Prohibited Conduct

### Adult and Sexual Content
- ❌ Displaying or discussing explicit sexual content
- ❌ Any form of sexual harassment
- ❌ Displaying nudity or sexual activity during real-person video calls
- ❌ Soliciting others for sexual activity

### Violence and Hate
- ❌ Threatening, intimidating, or harassing others
- ❌ Promoting violence, terrorism, or hate speech
- ❌ Bullying, doxxing, or online mob harassment

### Child Safety
- ❌ Any child sexual abuse material (immediate report to law enforcement)
- ❌ Grooming or soliciting minors for inappropriate conduct
- ❌ Unauthorized registration by minors without guardian consent

### Fraud and Illegal Activity
- ❌ Scamming, phishing, or account theft
- ❌ Selling prohibited goods or illegal services
- ❌ Money laundering or other financial crimes

### Intellectual Property
- ❌ Unauthorized sharing of copyrighted content belonging to others
- ❌ Unauthorized use of another person's likeness or voice to create an Exclusive Avatar

## 3. Exclusive Avatar — Special Rules

- You **must** use your own image and voice, or materials for which you have obtained explicit authorization
- Cloning the likeness of public figures, political figures, or other individuals without authorization is prohibited
- Using an Exclusive Avatar to impersonate a real person is prohibited
- Content generated by Exclusive Avatars must be labeled as "AI-Generated"

## 4. Real-Person Video Calls — Special Rules

- Camera is **off by default**; both parties must voluntarily enable it
- Recording or screenshotting of calls is prohibited
- Displaying illegal or prohibited content during calls is prohibited
- Using virtual cameras to falsify one's appearance is prohibited

## 5. Reporting

### How to Report
- **In-app reporting**: Tap the "Report" button in the call or chat interface
- **Email reporting**: report@qisuanai.com
- Please provide as much detail as possible (screenshots, time, counterparty account, etc.)

### Review Process
1. Report received → initial review within 24 hours
2. AI (Hive Moderation) + human dual review
3. Violation confirmed → content taken down within 24–48 hours
4. Severe violation → permanent account ban
5. Suspected illegality → reported to relevant law enforcement

### Appeals
If you believe a moderation decision was made in error, send an appeal to appeal@qisuanai.com.

## 6. Consequences of Violations

| Violation | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|
| 1st (minor) | Warning notice |
| 1st (severe) | 7-day feature restriction |
| 2nd | 30-day feature restriction |
| 3rd or egregious | Permanent account ban |

The Platform reserves the right of final determination regarding what constitutes a "minor" or "severe" violation, and may impose a permanent ban immediately based on the severity of the violation.

## 7. Disclaimer

Nira provides technical services only. Users are responsible for their own interactions. The Platform does not warrant the legality of user conduct but will make reasonable efforts to maintain community safety.
