Positive psychology is the scientific study of human strengths, well-being, and what makes life worth living.

Core concepts:
1. PERMA Model: Positive Emotion, Engagement, Relationships, Meaning, Accomplishment
2. Flow State: Optimal experience achieved when fully immersed in an activity
3. Character Strengths: 24 universal strengths including creativity, curiosity, perseverance, kindness, and leadership

Practical exercises:
- Three Good Things: Write down three good things daily and their causes for one week
- Gratitude Letter: Write and deliver a letter of thanks to someone who helped you
- Strengths Deployment: Identify your signature strengths and use them in new ways daily

Research shows happiness comes not from absence of pain, but from meaningful life and positive relationships.
