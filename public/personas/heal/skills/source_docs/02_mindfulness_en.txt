Mindfulness is the practice of paying attention to the present moment with intention and without judgment.

Core elements:
1. Intention: Consciously directing attention to present experience including breath, body sensations, emotions, and thoughts
2. Non-judgment: Observing without evaluating, accepting experiences as they are
3. Present moment: Gently returning attention to the here and now when the mind wanders

Benefits of mindfulness meditation:
- Stress reduction: Lower cortisol levels, reduced anxiety symptoms
- Improved focus: Enhanced prefrontal cortex activity, better attention and working memory
- Emotional regulation: Greater awareness of emotions, reduced reactivity
- Better sleep: Relaxation of body and mind, reduced insomnia
- Enhanced immunity: Reduced inflammation, improved immune cell activity

Daily practices:
- Mindful breathing: Focus on breath rhythm for 5 minutes
- Body scan: Progressively feel each body part from toes to head
- Mindful walking: Notice foot sensations, leg movement, and breath rhythm while walking
