The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People: Be proactive and take responsibility for your choices. Begin with the end in mind and clarify your goals before starting. Put first things first and spend time on what matters most. Think win-win and seek mutual benefit, not zero-sum outcomes. Seek first to understand, then to be understood. Synergize and value differences to create solutions greater than the sum of their parts. Sharpen the saw and continuously invest in your physical, spiritual, mental, and social-emotional capacities.
