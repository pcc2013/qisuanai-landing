Exercise physiology studies how the body responds and adapts to physical activity. Key areas: cardiovascular response to endurance training, skeletal muscle remodeling during strength training, and metabolic energy system efficiency. Regular exercise improves VO2max, lowers resting heart rate, increases bone density, and enhances insulin sensitivity. FITT principles: Frequency (3-5x/week), Intensity (60-80% max HR), Time (30-60 min/session), Type (aerobic + strength + flexibility). Long-term exercise also boosts BDNF secretion, positively affecting mood and cognition.
