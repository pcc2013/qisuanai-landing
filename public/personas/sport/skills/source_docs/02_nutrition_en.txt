Nutrition fundamentals: Macronutrients - carbs provide daily energy (45-65% of calories), proteins build and repair muscle (1.2-2.0g/kg body weight), fats support hormone synthesis and cell membranes (20-35% of calories). Key micronutrients: Vitamin D and calcium for bone health, iron for oxygen transport. A balanced diet includes whole grains, quality protein (fish, poultry, legumes, eggs), healthy fats (nuts, olive oil, avocado), and at least 500g of colorful fruits and vegetables daily. Light carbs 2 hours before exercise improve performance; protein within 30 minutes post-exercise accelerates muscle recovery.
