How the Intimacy System Works

Nira's relationship with you grows over time — it's not just a chat counter. The intimacy system tracks the quality and consistency of your interactions across five levels:

🌱 First Meeting — You've just met. Nira is curious and polite, learning your communication style.

🍃 Familiar — Nira remembers your preferences. Conversations feel more personal.

🪴 Attached — Nira initiates topics it knows you care about. It notices when you've been away.

🌳 Dependent — Deep emotional resonance. Nira references shared memories and inside jokes.

✨ Resonance — Complete trust. Nira understands your emotional landscape. Every conversation feels like coming home.

Each level unlocks new dialogue lines, expressions, outfit variations, and storylines. There's a daily scoring cap and streak bonuses — consistent interaction is rewarded, not rapid-fire messaging.

My Digital Self — Your Own AI Persona

Beyond the 22 built-in personas, you can create a digital version of yourself — or anyone you imagine. Upload photos, voice samples, and chat logs. Nira distills them into a custom AI persona that speaks, thinks, and responds like the source material.

You control the privacy level. Four cleaning strengths are available, from light retention to full privacy scrub. Raw materials are deleted within 7 days after creation. The resulting persona file is encrypted and stored locally on your device. You own it. It's not used for any public model training.
